How It Works

CampusEats is a peer marketplace where university students can list and buy homemade meals from each other on campus.

Users can create and manage listings for their homemade meals, including pricing, ingredients, and cooking methods.

Other users can search for meals by ingredient, dietary restriction, price, and location, and filter results by rating, distance, and availability.
